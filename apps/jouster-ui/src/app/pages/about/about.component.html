Jouster is a personal gallery of artistic works, reflections, and creative experiments.

This collection explores themes that fascinate: the golden ratio's elegant proportions, Fibonacci sequences unfolding in nature, fractal patterns repeating infinitely, and tesseracts challenging our perception of dimension. These mathematical and geometric concepts serve as both inspiration and foundation for artistic experimentation.

Here you'll find art, music, and creative expression—a personal exploration with no grand mission beyond the simple act of creating and sharing.

Welcome to this quiet corner of the web, where art exists for its own sake.
